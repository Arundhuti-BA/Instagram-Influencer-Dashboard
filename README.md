 <br> DETAILS OF THE DATASET:
 <br>In this file, basically there are 10 attributes. It has been ordered on basis of the rank
 which has been decided on basis of "followers".
 <br>● rank: Rank of the Influencer on basis of number of followers they have
<br>● channel_info: Username of the Instagrammer.
 <br>● influence score: Influence score of the users. It is calculated on basis of mentions,
 importance and popularity.
 <br>● posts: Number of posts they have made so far.
 <br>● followers: Number of followers of the user.
 <br>● avg_likes: Average likes on instagrammer posts (total likes/ total posts).
 <br>● 60_day_eng_rate: Last 60 days engagement rate of instagrammer as faction of
 engagements they have done so far.
 <br>● new_post_avg_like: Average likes they have on new posts.
<br>● total Likes: Total likes the user has got on their posts (in Billion).
<br>● country: Country or region of origin of the user.

<br>TASKS TO BE PERFORMED:
<br>● Defining Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)this will measure influencer performance and engagement.
<br>● Using histogram for distribution analysis and scatter plot to see corelation between follower size and average likes.
<br>● Ranking influencers to show the top 10 for each country and globally.
<br>● Performing a country wise analysis.
<br>● Creating four dashboard focusing on the following points: 
 1) Overview of Influencer Performanc
 2) Engagement and Influence Metrics
 3)  Country-Specific Insights
 4)  Engagement Trends

 Visualisation include following charts: KPI’s, Top 10 influencers based on influence_score with rank
 labels, geographic distribution of influencers by country color-coded by the average engagement rate.
